Welcome to diaBeaters!

Welcome to diaBeaters! diaBeaters! is an online intervention created for social work graduate students and practitioners to use with families living with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (T2DM) in a child family member.

Type 2 Diabetes, already a major health problem among adults, is the fastest growing pediatric epidemic of our decade. diaBeaters! provides a multi-stepped, online, interactive intervention for social work practitioners to guide parents and family caregivers to make optimal nutrition and fitness choices for their families and to teach their children appropriate health habits.

For additional information about this project, please contact CCNMTL at ccnmtl-diabeaters@columbia.edu.
